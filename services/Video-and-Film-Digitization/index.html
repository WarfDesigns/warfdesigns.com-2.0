How do I know if my Books or Music are in the Public Domain?


  • If works are published before 1928.
  • If certain works are explicitly dedicated to the public domain by the author.
  • If they are documents, reports, newspapers, etc.

Use reliable sources such as Project Gutenberg or HathiTrust to verify the public domain status of a book or music.
