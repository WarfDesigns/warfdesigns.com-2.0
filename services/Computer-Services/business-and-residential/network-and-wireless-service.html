Network & Wireless FAQ

Why is my Wi-Fi fast in one room but slow in another?

Walls, distance, and interference from neighboring networks can weaken your signal. We measure and correct these issues with placement, channels, and access points.

Do I need mesh Wi-Fi?

If you have dead zones or a larger multi-story home, mesh or additional access points often deliver the best coverage and stability.

Can you secure my network for guests and smart devices?

Yes. We set up guest networks, isolate IoT devices, enforce strong encryption, and implement DNS/web filtering if desired.

Will you work with my existing ISP equipment?

Absolutely. We can optimize what you have or recommend better hardware for your goals and budget.
